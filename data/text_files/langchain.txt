
Introduction to LangChain

LangChain is an open-source framework designed for building applications powered by Large Language Models (LLMs).

Core Components:
- Document Loaders
- Text Splitters
- Embedding Models
- Vector Stores
- Retrievers
- Chains
- Agents

LangChain simplifies the development of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems by connecting
LLMs with external knowledge sources such as PDFs, databases, and APIs.
